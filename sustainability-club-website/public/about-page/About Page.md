# **About Our Carbon Calculator**

## **Our Mission**

Our mission is to make carbon footprint tracking simple, accessible, and actionable. Understanding your environmental impact should not require complicated dashboards, lengthy explanations, or account creation. We believe sustainability tools should be easy to use while remaining scientifically rigorous.

## **Why This Matters**

Climate change is a global challenge that requires both systemic change and individual awareness. Many carbon calculators are either overly complex or require users to create accounts before accessing results. This creates friction and discourages engagement.

Our platform removes those barriers. You can enter your information and calculate your footprint instantly — no login required.

## **What Makes Our Calculator Different**

* **No login or account required**

* **Minimal, distraction-free interface**

* **Comprehensive calculations behind a simple design**

* **Integrated research forum for idea-sharing and discussion**

Our core advantage is simplicity. While the interface is streamlined, the calculations are built on established carbon accounting principles, including energy conversion factors, lifecycle emissions, and efficiency adjustments.

We balance depth with usability.

## **How It Works**

The calculator estimates emissions by applying standard emission factors to user inputs such as energy use, transportation distance, food consumption, product purchases, and digital activity.

Both direct emissions (like fuel or electricity use) and indirect lifecycle emissions (such as manufacturing impacts) are considered where appropriate.

All results are presented clearly to help users understand where their largest impacts occur and where changes can be made.

## **Transparency & Limitations**

All results are estimates based on average emission factors and publicly available environmental data. Actual emissions may vary depending on location, infrastructure, and individual behavior.

This tool is intended for education, awareness, and informed decision-making.

## **About the Creators**

This project was co-created and started by **Max Zong and Derek Liu**, students at Westminster School in Connecticut and leaders of their school’s Sustainability Club. The website is maintained by members in their school's Sustainability Club. Their goal is to extend their environmental impact beyond their campus by building accessible digital tools that encourage climate awareness and action.

In addition to the calculator, the platform includes a research forum where users can share ideas, propose solutions, and engage in thoughtful discussion about sustainability.
